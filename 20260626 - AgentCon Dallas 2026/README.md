## About the Event

AgentCon World Tour is a free, community-driven conference series for developers, engineers, and researchers who are actively building with AI agents.

The Dallas stop is a one-day deep dive into the real challenges of designing, deploying, and integrating autonomous AI systems. Expect hands-on demos, technical sessions, and practical conversations from people building agents in the field.

Whether you are shipping your first agent or scaling production systems, AgentCon is designed for builders who want practical knowledge, real-world patterns, and a stronger local AI community.

## What to Expect

- Deep-dive talks from AI practitioners and community leaders
- Technical workshops on building, deploying, and scaling agents
- Live demos of open-source frameworks and tools
- Two focused tracks covering technical implementation and high-impact panels
- DFW networking with engineers, mentors, hiring teams, and AI builders
